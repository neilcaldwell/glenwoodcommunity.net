Welcome

Welcome to the Glenwood Community Association (GCA), NSW. We are a non-partisan non-sectarian association dedicated to improving the amenity of Glenwood. Thinking globally and acting locally, we remove litter and promote recycling, revegetation, renewable energy, better town planning and more. Posts that are unrelated to these objectives will be deleted. A separate forum will be created for buying, selling and swapping goods.

Join the GCA by completing this application form (http://goo.gl/forms/fyChjHnJMi) or emailing president@glenwoodcommunity.net
